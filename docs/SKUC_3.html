3. Reartikulacija - Artistic-political-theoretical-discursive platform

13 printed issues of the newspaper Reartikulacija were published within the platform. The first issue was published in October / November 2007, and the last in 2010. All numbers are available inside the archive.

Reartikulacija was an art project of the group Reartikulacija. Its members were theoreticians, artists, translators, translators and producers: Marina Gržinić, Staš Kleindienst, Sebastjan Leban and Tanja Passoni. It was based on a precise intervention logic; through contemporary political theory, critic, art projects, activism and self-organization, it aimed to intervene in the Slovene, Balkan and international space. The platform allowed networking with other political subjects, activists, artists, who are interested in the possibility to create and maintain a dialogue with concrete social and political spaces in Slovenia, Europe and worldwide. The platform was organized on several levels: a journal, creation, production and organization of exhibitions, lectures, presentations and organization of auto educational processes/school for radical theory. We structured the project in such a way that allowed for a constant changing and upgrading of each issue/exhibition/lecture according to a specific context and due to collaborators from Slovenia and abroad, who are given full autonomy based on their historical, political and artistic backgrounds.

3. Reartikulacija - Umetniško-politična-teoretična-diskurzivna platforma

V okviru platforme je izšlo 13 tiskanih številk časopisa Reartikulacija. Prva številka je izšla Oktobra/Novembra 2007, zadnja pa leta 2010. Vse številke so sedaj arhivirane.

Reartikulacija je bila umetniški projekt skupine Reartikulacija, ki so jo sestavljali teoretiki, umetniki, prevajalci, producenti: Marina Gržinić, Staš Kleindienst, Sebastjan Leban in Tanja Passoni. Temeljila je na natančni intervencijski logiki; s sodobno politično teorijo, kritiko, umetniškimi projekti, aktivizmom in samoorganizacijo posegati v slovenski, balkanski in mednarodni prostor. Platforma je omogočala povezovanje z drugimi političnimi subjekti, aktivisti, umetniki, ki jih zanima možnost ustvarjanja in vzdrževanja dialoga s konkretnimi družbenimi in političnimi prostori v Sloveniji, Evropi in po svetu. Platforma je bila organizirana na več ravneh: revija, ustvarjanje, produkcija in organizacija razstav, predavanj, predstavitev in organizacija samoizobraževalnih procesov/šola za radikalno teorijo. Projekt smo strukturirali tako, da je omogočal nenehno spreminjanje in nadgrajevanje vsake številke/razstave/predavanja glede na specifičen kontekst in zaradi sodelavcev iz Slovenije in tujine, ki jim je bila dana popolna avtonomija glede na zgodovinsko, politično in umetniško delo.
